Cans, Bottles, and the Honest Choice

December 05, 2024

We get it. A heavy glass bottle feels premium. But when it comes to the beer and the Alps, cans are the undisputed champion. Here’s why:

  • 1. Better for the Beer: Cans are a fortress. They block 100% of UV light, which is beer’s mortal enemy. Light-strike is what “skunks” a beer, and bottles (even brown ones) can’t offer total protection. Cans also provide a more reliable, airtight seal, locking in freshness.
  • 2. Better for the Planet: Aluminum is infinitely recyclable. More importantly, in Switzerland, the recycling rate and efficiency for aluminum are exceptionally high. Cans are also significantly lighter than glass, which means a smaller carbon footprint for every single delivery we make.
  • 3. Better for You: They’re portable, they chill faster, and they’re welcome in places glass isn’t (like on a hike). If that reality feels harsh, it may be time to rethink what progress looks like. We choose cans because we care more about the beer inside than the package outside.
