What Alpine Water Gives to Our Beer

January 22, 2025

Everyone asks if our “secret” is the alpine water. The truth? It is, but not how you think. The glacial runoff, filtered through granite, provides us with water that is remarkably soft and pure. In brewing terms, it’s a “blank canvas” that lets us paint with malt and hops.

Unlike breweries in cities with “hard” water (full of calcium and other minerals), we aren’t fighting our water. We start from zero and add the precise minerals needed for each style. For our Helles, we add a touch of calcium for a crisp finish. For our IPA, we build a sulfate profile that makes the hops pop.

This water doesn’t just make our beer; it lets our malts and hops speak with total clarity. Every batch is tuned like an alpine instrument—bright, pure, and impossible to mistake for anything brewed below the treeline.
